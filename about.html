The Origin Story

From a tiny 5-member chatterbox to a weightless community of 75+ legends. Here is how we got here.

Est. June 2012

It started with a single notification. Back in the summer of 2012, a small group of friends decided that standard group chats were too quiet. They needed a dedicated sanctuary for high-energy conversations, unfiltered memes, and spontaneous weekend road trips. The Masti Mongsters WhatsApp group was born.

What began as a place to coordinate weekend dinners quickly evolved. Friends invited friends, memes turned into inside jokes, and casual hangouts turned into epic annual meetups. The rule was simple: bring positive energy, keep the conversation active, and respect the pack.

Today, the community is a bustling digital ecosystem of 75+ active members. Spanning across different cities, careers, and timezones, the Masti Mongsters remains anchored by its core spirit of friendship, celebration, and endless banter.

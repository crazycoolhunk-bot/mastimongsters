Community Guidelines

Every pack has its rules to keep the vibes active. These are the core rules that govern our 75+ members.

🤝

Respect the Pack

Treat every member like family. No personal attacks, sensitive disclosures, or offensive speech. Disagreements are handled offline, keeping the main space harmonious.

⚡

Keep the Masti Alive

Active participation keeps our heartbeat alive. Post funny stickers, start banter, coordinate weekend hangouts, and check in on each other. A silent group is a dead group.

01

No Generic Forward Spam

Avoid sending bulk forwarded messages, circulars, good morning cards, or external commercial ads. Keep shared content authentic, custom, and meme-worthy.

02

Active Participation

Lurking is fine, but try to pitch in sometimes! Check in on polls, react to plans, and send updates. If you are going offline for a digital detox, drop a quick heads-up.

03

Meetup RSVP Integrity

We host meetups to build real-world bonds. Participate in planning polls. If you RSVP 'Yes' to an event, make every effort to show up. Avoid late cancellations unless it's a genuine emergency.

04

Strict Privacy Control

What happens in Masti Mongsters stays in Masti Mongsters. Never screenshot group conversations, profile links, or media files to share with external parties without explicit consent.

05

Moderator Mandate

Group Admins reserve the right to mute or remove members who repeatedly spam, provoke arguments, or break guidelines. Respect their decisions; they keep the peace.
